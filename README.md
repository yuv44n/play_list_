# YouTube Playlist Progress Tracker

A simple web app to track your progress through YouTube playlists. Never lose your place in long tutorial series again!

## How to Use

### Step 1: Start a New Task List
When you first open the app, you'll see a clean interface with a "Start New Task List" button.

<img width="1038" height="220" alt="Screenshot 2025-10-04 024315" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e9f37b0e-ea4b-4e50-a876-2a5ae86b5c36" />


### Step 2: Enter Your YouTube Playlist URL
A popup will appear asking for your YouTube playlist URL. Simply paste the link to any public YouTube playlist.

<img width="932" height="446" alt="Screenshot 2025-10-04 024334" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cbed95ff-3cb7-4e43-8e29-f7345a6cb7b4" />


### Step 3: Track Your Progress
The app will load all videos from the playlist and display them as a checklist. Click on any video to mark it as completed, and watch your progress update in real-time!

<img width="1091" height="959" alt="Screenshot 2025-10-04 024403" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/624f5924-d957-4853-9a1f-08b5eaae02a7" />

Click videos to mark as complete • Progress bar updates automatically • Your progress is saved locally
